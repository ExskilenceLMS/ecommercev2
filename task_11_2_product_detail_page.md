# Task 11 – Subtask 11.2: Product Details Page

## 1. Overview

This subtask introduces the **Product Details Page**, which allows users to **view complete information about a specific product**.  
It provides detailed product data and serves as a bridge between product discovery and purchasing actions.

This subtask builds directly on the Product Listing Page introduced in Task 11.1.

---

## 2. Purpose

The purpose of this subtask is to:

- Display detailed information for individual products
- Provide users with clarity before making purchase decisions
- Support navigation from product listing to purchasing flows
- Enhance overall product visibility and presentation

---

## 3. Prerequisites

Before completing this subtask, the following should already be implemented:

- Product listing page (Task 11.1)
- Product data model and relationships
- Category management
- Product blueprint structure
- Base layout template

---

## 4. Product Details Features

This subtask includes the following capabilities:

- Display detailed product information (name, description, price, category)
- Show product availability or stock status
- Provide a clear product-focused layout
- Ensure product data is presented accurately and consistently

---

## 5. Access Control

- The product details page is **publicly accessible**
- No authentication is required to view product details
- Only active and available products are displayed

This ensures transparency and ease of access for all users.

---

## 6. Implementation Summary

- Product details logic is defined within the product blueprint
- Product data is retrieved using a unique product identifier
- Templates extend the shared base layout for UI consistency
- Code related to this subtask is clearly marked for maintainability

---

## 7. Scope and Limitations

**Included in this subtask:**
- Product details view
- Display of essential product attributes

**Not included in this subtask:**
- Add-to-cart functionality
- Product reviews or ratings
- Related product recommendations
- Inventory reservation logic

These features are implemented in subsequent tasks.

---

## 8. Outcome

After completing this subtask:

- Users can view complete product information
- Product pages support informed purchasing decisions
- The system flows smoothly from browsing to cart actions
- The application is ready for cart and checkout features
